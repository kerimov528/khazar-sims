Completion Exam Application (Qiym…ôt Completion Test)

After the grades are transferred to your transcripts at the end of the semester, you can apply for the completion exams of your courses with 57, 58, 59 grades via this menu.

You can apply within 5 days after the grades are transferred to the Transcript.

Previously made applications and finalized applications can also be followed through this screen.
