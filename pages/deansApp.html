Application to the Dean s Office

You can send your requests after ers registration or any other request to the Dean s Office via this menu. The dean s officer will follow them and give you the appropriate answers.

Changes can be made on the unanswered application, and your application date will be renewed with the change. If the application has been answered, you cannot make changes on the application, you can follow the response and the date on the screen.
