Enrolling for the Course

You can make your course registrations through this menu.

On the left side of the screen, the courses you can register for are listed. At the bottom of the list, there are explanations for the course categories.

On the right side of the screen; At the top, you can see the ECTS limit you can enroll in and the ECTS you have written. Below it, the syllabus of the courses you have chosen is displayed. Below are the courses you have chosen and their color codes. At the bottom, the courses you have already taken and their grades are displayed. Courses with a black background are prerequisite and you cannot take them.

On the selection screen (left side); The courses of the current semester are painted. Classes for which the quota of the group is completed are closed. If you choose a group of a course, it is closed so that you do not choose the other groups incorrectly. When you hover over the "eye" sign on the far right of the list, you can see its place in the curriculum.

If you try to choose courses that you are not allowed to take as per the regulations, the system will warn you. You will not be allowed to exceed your ECTS limits.

If you think there is an error in your courses or grades, it would be appropriate to contact the Registrar's Office.
